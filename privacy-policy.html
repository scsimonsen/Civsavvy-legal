CivSavvy Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026 Last updated: June 11, 2026

Civic LLC ("CivSavvy," "we," "us," or "our") operates the CivSavvy mobile application and related services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

If you have questions, contact us at sorencsimonsen@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information. Email address and password when you create an account. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Profile information. Your name and, if you provide it, your street address, city, state, and ZIP code. We use your address or location to determine your congressional, state legislative, and county districts so we can show you relevant legislation and representatives.
  • Interests and preferences. Policy topics you select, notification preferences, and similar settings.
  • Content you create. Bills you follow, arguments and notes you save, and other content you generate in the app.
  • Payment information. If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Apple (in-app purchase) or Stripe. We never receive or store your full card number. We receive limited transaction data (e.g., subscription status, plan, and transaction identifiers) to manage your subscription.

Information collected automatically

  • Location data. With your permission, we collect your device's precise location (GPS coordinates) during onboarding to determine your voting districts. You may instead enter an address manually. We use location at that moment to derive your districts; you can update or remove your address in settings.
  • Usage data. How you interact with the Service, such as bills viewed or followed, civic events viewed or shared, and feature engagement. We use this internally to operate and improve the Service. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • Device and push data. A push notification token (via Expo) if you enable notifications, and basic device information needed to deliver the Service.

Information from other sources

We obtain publicly available civic data — legislation, legislator information, voting records, and elections data — from government and public sources. This is public information about officials, not personal information about you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: create and manage your account; determine your voting districts and show you relevant legislation and representatives; deliver features such as followed bills, saved arguments, and personalized civic updates; send push notifications you have opted into; process and manage subscriptions; respond to support requests; maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse; and comply with legal obligations.

We use aggregated or de-identified data to understand usage and improve the Service.

AI processing. Summaries and analyses of legislation shown in the app are generated on our servers using third-party AI services (Anthropic). This processing runs on public legislative text — your personal information is not sent to AI services to generate this content.

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties for their advertising or marketing.
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks or cross-app tracking.
  • We do not use your data to build profiles for purposes unrelated to the Service.
  • We do not share your political interests, followed bills, or saved content with campaigns, parties, or data brokers.

4. How We Share Information

We share personal information only with:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations: Supabase (database and authentication hosting), Stripe (payment processing), Apple (in-app purchases), and Expo (push notification delivery).
  • Legal authorities when required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of CivSavvy, our users, or others.
  • A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case this Privacy Policy will continue to apply to your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information while your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we must retain limited records to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations, or to resolve disputes. De-identified usage data may be retained.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, correction, deletion. You can view and update your profile in the app. You can delete your account and associated data in the app (Settings) or by emailing us at sorencsimonsen@gmail.com.
  • Location. You can decline location permission and enter an address manually, or revoke location permission in your device settings at any time.
  • Push notifications. Opt out in the app or your device settings.
  • State privacy rights. Depending on your state of residence (including California, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Virginia, and others), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of your personal information, and to opt out of certain processing. We honor these requests for all users regardless of state. To exercise any right, email sorencsimonsen@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days. We do not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. If we deny a request, you may appeal by replying to our decision.
  • California residents. We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA, and we do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes requiring a right to limit.
  • EEA/UK users. If you use the Service from the EEA or UK, we process your data on the legal bases of contract performance, legitimate interests, and consent (for location). You have rights under the GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, and objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS), encrypted storage, hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and will notify you of any breach as required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Sensitive Information

Your district, followed bills, and topic interests may reveal political views. We treat this data with heightened care: it is used only to deliver the Service to you, is never sold or shared for marketing, and is deleted with your account.

10. International Users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access it from elsewhere, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the app or by email before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
